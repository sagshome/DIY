Portfolios

Portfolios are used to group together accounts. They are useful for

  • Chaining Accounts - When you move an account from/to a different provider
  • Linking Accounts - Multiple active accounts form a common target.

    • The only data associated with a portfolio is its name (which must be unique) and the currency to report in, which can be different then
